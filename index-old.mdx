## What is AWO?

AWO is a **mobile-first wealth management platform** that combines behavioral financial analysis with culturally relevant community features. Our mission is to democratize access to savings, investment, and financial education for African women.

### Key Innovation: DIVA Scoring System

Our proprietary **DIVA Score** (Discipline, Income, Velocity, Assets) analyzes real bank transaction data to provide personalized financial health assessments and recommendations.

## Core Features

### 1. DIVA Scoring System

Our proprietary financial health scoring system that analyzes:

- **Discipline**: Payment punctuality and consistency
- **Income**: Stability, growth, and diversification
- **Velocity**: Cash flow patterns and efficiency
- **Assets**: Accumulation, protection, and optimization

### 2. Digital Chama/Stokvel Management

Modernized traditional savings groups with:

- Blockchain-backed transparency
- Automated contribution tracking
- Smart contract-based payouts
- Group communication tools

### 3. Financial Education Hub

Gamified learning platform featuring:

- Bite-sized educational modules
- Progress tracking and certifications
- Community discussions
- Localized content for each market

### 4. Investment Marketplace

Curated investment opportunities including:

- Micro-investment options
- Risk-appropriate product matching
- Performance tracking
- Educational content for each investment type
