# Financial Compliance

AWO Platform operates within a comprehensive financial compliance framework designed to meet the highest standards of anti-money laundering (AML), know your customer (KYC), banking regulations, and investment management requirements across the SADC region. Our approach ensures financial integrity while supporting financial inclusion for African women.

## Financial Compliance Overview

<Info>
AWO Platform's financial compliance framework implements a risk-based approach aligned with FATF recommendations, SADC regional standards, and local regulatory requirements to ensure robust protection against financial crime while enabling innovative financial services.
</Info>

## Know Your Customer (KYC) Framework

### Tiered KYC Approach

AWO Platform implements a progressive, risk-based KYC framework that balances financial inclusion with regulatory compliance:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Tier 1 KYC - Basic Access" icon="id-badge">
    **Requirements**: Mobile number verification + basic identity information  
    **Verification**: ID/passport number verification against government databases  
    **Access Level**: AWO Wallet, P2P transfers (&lt;R1,000 per transaction)  
    **Transaction Limits**: R5,000 monthly transaction limit  
    **Regulatory Basis**: Simplified Customer Due Diligence under FATF recommendations
  </Card>
  <Card title="Tier 2 KYC - Enhanced Access" icon="id-card-alt">
    **Requirements**: Document verification + biometric verification + bank linking  
    **Verification**: Smile ID document authentication + liveness detection  
    **Access Level**: Full platform access including investments and Chama management  
    **Transaction Limits**: Standard limits based on risk assessment  
    **Regulatory Basis**: Full Customer Due Diligence compliance
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

**KYC Documentation and Verification**:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Identity Document Verification">
    **Accepted Documents**: National ID, passport, driver's license per jurisdiction  
    **Document Authentication**: Smile ID authentication with anti-fraud detection  
    **Data Extraction**: Automated data extraction with manual verification backup  
    **Document Retention**: Secure encrypted storage with appropriate retention periods
  </Step>
  <Step title="Biometric Verification">
    **Liveness Detection**: Advanced liveness detection to prevent spoofing  
    **Facial Recognition**: Facial recognition matching against identity documents  
    **Voice Verification**: Optional voice verification for enhanced security  
    **Continuous Authentication**: Behavioral biometrics for ongoing authentication
  </Step>
  <Step title="Address and Contact Verification">
    **Address Verification**: Utility bills, bank statements, or government correspondence  
    **Mobile Number Verification**: SMS OTP verification for mobile number confirmation  
    **Email Verification**: Email confirmation with secure links  
    **Contact Information Updates**: Secure procedures for updating contact information
  </Step>
  <Step title="Source of Funds Verification">
    **Employment Verification**: Salary slips, employment letters, or tax certificates  
    **Business Income**: Business registration, tax returns, or financial statements  
    **Investment Income**: Portfolio statements or investment confirmations  
    **Bank Account Verification**: Open banking verification of account ownership and activity
  </Step>
</Steps>
