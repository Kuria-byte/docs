# Audit Procedures

AWO Platform maintains a comprehensive audit framework that ensures regulatory compliance, operational effectiveness, and risk management across all SADC markets. This framework encompasses internal audit functions, external audit requirements, continuous monitoring systems, and systematic remediation procedures designed to maintain the highest standards of governance and accountability.

## Audit Framework Overview

<Info>
AWO Platform's audit framework implements a risk-based, three lines of defense model that provides comprehensive assurance on the effectiveness of governance, risk management, and control processes while supporting business objectives and regulatory compliance.
</Info>

### Three Lines of Defense Model

**Integrated Assurance Framework**:
AWO Platform's audit procedures are built on the internationally recognized three lines of defense model, ensuring comprehensive coverage and clear accountability:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="First Line - Management Controls" icon="users">
    **Ownership**: Business management owns and manages risks and controls  
    **Responsibility**: Direct responsibility for identifying, assessing, and managing risks  
    **Implementation**: Implementation and operation of control measures  
    **Self-Assessment**: Regular self-assessment and monitoring of control effectiveness
  </Card>
  <Card title="Second Line - Risk and Compliance" icon="shield-check">
    **Oversight**: Independent oversight of first line risk and control activities  
    **Support**: Support and challenge to first line on risk and compliance matters  
    **Monitoring**: Monitoring and reporting on risk and compliance performance  
    **Policy**: Development and maintenance of risk and compliance policies
  </Card>
  <Card title="Third Line - Internal Audit" icon="magnifying-glass">
    **Assurance**: Independent assurance on effectiveness of governance, risk, and control  
    **Evaluation**: Objective evaluation of first and second line effectiveness  
    **Reporting**: Independent reporting to board and senior management  
    **Recommendations**: Recommendations for improvement and enhancement
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Internal Audit Framework

**Board-Approved Audit Charter**:
AWO Platform's internal audit function operates under a comprehensive charter that ensures independence, authority, and accountability:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Independence and Objectivity">
    **Organizational Independence**: Internal audit reports functionally to the board audit committee  
    **Direct Access**: Direct access to board, senior management, and all business areas  
    **Objectivity**: Objective and unbiased approach to all audit activities

  <Step title="Authority and Scope">

</Steps>
